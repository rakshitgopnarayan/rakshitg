Habitual

(App Logo)

Designing The Logo

Since the app is an e-commerce app there needed to be some inclusion of illustration relating to the theme of shopping. It could either be a shopping bag, shopping cart, or something completely different. Most famous e-commerce stores in India are Amazon and Flipkart, both of which have a cart in the logo. But, to make something easily identifiable yet unique I decided to inculcate the shopping cart logo into the typography (specifically “H”)  .

Choosing Color Theme

I decided to choose a single color scheme for the logo since the logo is a name without any separate icon. A green color variant suited best for the design and gave it a unique feel to it. Some of the variants I came up with are listed. 

Designing "H"

This was a challenging step because the goal was to implement cart into the typography but at the same time the letter “H” needs to be identified clearly. 
I came up with two variants : 

One with bridge line higher (closer to letter “H”)  

Second with bridge line lower(closer to a shopping cart) 

Typography

The font needed to be wider and less heighted to incorporate the previous design choices of including a shopping cart at the place of letter “H”. “Krona One” was the perfect font for the job.  

Alignment

I had two variations to work with at the end.  

Either align the handle of the cart perfectly with the slant height of “A”. Here there is a chance that the letter “H” might not be identified easily but the logo overall looks aesthetically fitting.

1.

Or increase the angle of the handle at the risk of it sticking out like a sour thumb. I decided to go with the former version as the positives outweighed the negatives.
